# Referral Program

## How It Works

Earn cash rewards by referring friends and family to Nova. When your referral opens an account and meets the qualifying criteria, both you and your referral receive a bonus.

## Current Offer

- **You get:** $25 credited to your Nova account
- **Your friend gets:** $25 credited to their new Nova account

## Qualifying Criteria

Your referral must:
1. Be a new Nova customer (never had a Nova account before)
2. Open an account using your unique referral link or code
3. Receive a qualifying direct deposit of $200 or more within 45 days of account opening

## How to Refer

1. Go to Home > Refer a Friend (or Settings > Referrals)
2. Share your unique referral link via text, email, or social media
3. Or share your referral code — your friend enters it during sign-up

## Tracking Referrals

View the status of all your referrals at Settings > Referrals > My Referrals:
- **Pending:** Your friend signed up but hasn't completed the qualifying criteria yet
- **Completed:** Your friend met the criteria and bonuses have been credited
- **Expired:** Your friend didn't meet the criteria within 45 days

## Limits

- Maximum 20 referral bonuses per calendar year ($500 total)
- Referral bonuses are considered income and may be reported on a 1099-MISC if total referral earnings exceed $600 in a calendar year
- Self-referrals are not allowed
- The referral program may be modified or discontinued at any time

## Bonus Payout

Referral bonuses are credited within 3 business days of your referral meeting the qualifying criteria. The bonus appears as "Nova Referral Bonus" in your transaction history.
