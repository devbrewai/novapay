# International Transfers

## Supported Countries

Nova supports international transfers to over 50 countries, including the United Kingdom, Canada, Mexico, Germany, France, Japan, Australia, India, the Philippines, Nigeria, and Brazil. For a full list of supported countries, go to Send Money > International > Supported Countries.

## How to Send an International Transfer

1. Open the Nova app and tap "Send Money"
2. Select "International Transfer"
3. Enter the recipient's details:
   - Full legal name (as it appears on their bank account)
   - Bank name and SWIFT/BIC code
   - Account number or IBAN
   - Recipient's country and currency
4. Enter the amount in USD — the converted amount is shown automatically
5. Review the exchange rate, fees, and total cost
6. Confirm with your PIN or biometric authentication

## Processing Times

- **Express (1-2 business days):** Available for most major currencies. Additional $5 fee for Standard accounts; free for Premium accounts.
- **Standard (3-5 business days):** Default option. Fee structure per your account type.
- **Economy (5-7 business days):** Available for select corridors. 0.5% lower fee than standard.

## Exchange Rates

Nova uses the mid-market exchange rate (the rate you see on Google or XE.com) plus a 0.5% markup. The exchange rate is locked for 30 seconds after it's displayed, giving you time to review and confirm.

## Transfer Limits

- Standard accounts: $2,500/day, $5,000/month
- Premium accounts: $10,000/day, $50,000/month

## Cancellation

International transfers can be cancelled within 30 minutes of submission, provided the transfer has not been picked up by the recipient's bank. Go to Transactions > find the transfer > Cancel. After 30 minutes, cancellation is not guaranteed.
