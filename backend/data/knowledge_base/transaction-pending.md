# Pending Transactions

## What Is a Pending Transaction?

A pending transaction is a charge that has been authorized but not yet fully processed. When you make a purchase with your Nova card, the merchant's bank sends an authorization request. Nova approves the request and puts a temporary hold on your funds.

## How Long Do Transactions Stay Pending?

- **Most purchases:** 1-3 business days
- **Gas stations:** Up to 3 business days (gas station pre-authorizations are typically $1 initially, then updated to the actual amount)
- **Hotels and car rentals:** Up to 7 business days (these merchants often hold an estimated amount plus a deposit)
- **Restaurants:** 1-2 business days (the final amount may differ from the initial authorization if you add a tip)

## Impact on Your Balance

Pending transactions reduce your available balance but do not appear as completed transactions. Your account shows two balances:

- **Current balance:** Your total funds, not accounting for pending transactions
- **Available balance:** Your current balance minus all pending holds. This is the amount you can actually spend.

## Can I Cancel a Pending Transaction?

Nova cannot cancel pending transactions because they are controlled by the merchant's bank. However:

- If the merchant has not yet captured the funds, you can contact the merchant directly to request cancellation
- If the pending charge does not match what you expected, wait for it to complete and then file a dispute
- Pending authorizations that are not captured by the merchant typically expire and are released after 7 business days

## Pending Transaction Disappeared?

If a pending transaction disappears from your history, it means the merchant did not capture the funds within the authorization window. The hold is released and the funds are returned to your available balance. This is normal and no action is needed.

## Tips

- Always check your available balance (not current balance) before making large purchases
- If a pending charge seems incorrect, wait for it to complete before disputing
- Contact the merchant directly if you need to cancel a pending charge
