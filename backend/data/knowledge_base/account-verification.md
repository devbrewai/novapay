# Account Verification

## Identity Verification (KYC)

Nova is required by federal law to verify the identity of all account holders. This process is known as Know Your Customer (KYC) and happens when you first open your account.

## What You Need

- Full legal name (as it appears on your ID)
- Date of birth
- Social Security number (SSN)
- A valid government-issued photo ID:
  - U.S. driver's license
  - U.S. state-issued ID
  - U.S. passport or passport card
  - Permanent resident card (green card)

## Verification Process

**Instant verification (most common):** Your identity is verified automatically within 5 minutes using our secure verification partner. You will receive a confirmation email and can start using your account immediately.

**Manual review:** In some cases, automatic verification may not be possible. If this happens:
- You will receive an email requesting additional documentation
- Upload a clear photo of the front and back of your government ID
- You may be asked for a proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement dated within the last 90 days)
- Manual reviews are completed within 1-2 business days

## Verification Status

Check your verification status at any time: Settings > Account > Verification Status.

**Status meanings:**
- **Verified** — Your identity has been confirmed. No action needed.
- **Pending** — Verification is in progress. You may have limited access to certain features.
- **Action Required** — Additional documentation is needed. Check your email for instructions.
- **Denied** — Verification could not be completed. Contact support for assistance.

## Unverified Account Limitations

Until your identity is verified, the following limits apply:
- Maximum account balance: $1,000
- Daily transfer limit: $500
- No physical card ordering
- No international transfers

Once verified, your full account limits are restored based on your account type (Standard or Premium).
