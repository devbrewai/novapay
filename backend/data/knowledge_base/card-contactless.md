# Contactless Payments

## Apple Pay

Add your Nova card to Apple Pay for contactless payments with your iPhone, Apple Watch, iPad, or Mac.

**Setup:**
1. Open the Apple Wallet app
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Select "Debit or Credit Card"
4. Scan your Nova card or enter the details manually
5. Verify with Nova (you may receive a verification code via SMS or push notification)
6. Your card is ready to use with Apple Pay

## Google Pay

Add your Nova card to Google Pay for contactless payments on Android devices.

**Setup:**
1. Open the Google Pay app
2. Tap "Add a payment method"
3. Scan your Nova card or enter the details manually
4. Verify with Nova
5. Your card is ready to use

## How to Pay Contactless

**In stores:** Hold your phone or watch near the payment terminal. Look for the contactless payment symbol (four curved lines). Authenticate with Face ID, fingerprint, or your device PIN.

**Online:** Select Apple Pay or Google Pay at checkout. No need to enter card details.

## Contactless Limits

- No per-transaction limit for contactless payments authenticated with biometrics
- Some merchants may require PIN for contactless transactions over $100
- Your standard account spending limits still apply

## Security

Contactless payments through Apple Pay and Google Pay are highly secure:
- Your actual card number is never shared with the merchant
- Each transaction uses a unique, one-time security code
- Requires biometric authentication (Face ID, fingerprint) or device PIN
- If your phone is lost, you can remotely disable Apple Pay or Google Pay through your device's "Find My" feature

## Samsung Pay

Samsung Pay is also supported with the same setup process through the Samsung Pay app on compatible Samsung devices.
