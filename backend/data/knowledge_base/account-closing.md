# Closing Your Nova Account

## Before You Close

Before closing your Nova account, please ensure the following:

- Your account balance is $0, or you have a linked external bank account to transfer remaining funds
- All pending transactions have been completed or cancelled
- Any recurring payments or direct deposits have been redirected to another account
- You have downloaded any statements or tax documents you may need

## How to Request Account Closure

Account closures require verification from our team for security purposes. You cannot close your account through the app's self-service settings.

**To close your account:**

1. Contact Nova support through the chat widget or call 1-800-NOVA
2. Verify your identity (you'll be asked security questions or to confirm via 2FA)
3. A support specialist will guide you through the closure process
4. If you have a remaining balance, choose where to transfer it:
   - ACH transfer to an external bank account (free, 1-3 business days)
   - Mailed check to your address on file (free, 7-10 business days)

## What Happens After Closure

- Your physical and virtual cards are immediately deactivated
- Your account number and routing number are retired and cannot be reused
- You will receive a final statement via email within 5 business days
- Transaction history is retained for 7 years per federal regulations
- Any remaining cashback rewards are forfeited unless redeemed before closure

## Cooling-Off Period

After requesting closure, there is a 14-day cooling-off period during which you can cancel the closure request. After 14 days, the closure is final and irreversible.

## Reopening an Account

If your account has been closed, you may open a new Nova account at any time. However, your previous account number, transaction history, and settings cannot be restored.
