# Account Settings

## Accessing Your Settings

Open the Nova app and tap the gear icon in the bottom navigation bar, or go to Settings from the sidebar menu on desktop.

## Personal Information

**Display name:** Change how your name appears in the app. This does not affect your legal name on file. Go to Settings > Profile > Display Name.

**Email address:** Update your email at Settings > Profile > Email. You will need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

**Phone number:** Change your phone number at Settings > Profile > Phone Number. A verification code will be sent to the new number.

**Mailing address:** Update at Settings > Profile > Address. This affects where physical cards and paper statements are sent.

## Notification Preferences

Manage your notifications at Settings > Notifications:

- **Transaction alerts:** Get notified for every purchase, transfer, or deposit. Options: push notification, email, SMS, or all three.
- **Large transaction alerts:** Set a threshold amount (default: $100). Receive an alert for any transaction above this amount.
- **Weekly summary:** Receive a weekly spending summary every Monday morning.
- **Security alerts:** Login from new devices, password changes, and suspicious activity. These cannot be disabled for security reasons.
- **Marketing:** Product updates, new features, and promotional offers. Can be disabled.

## Display Settings

- **Currency display:** Choose between USD symbol ($) or ISO code (USD). Default: $.
- **Date format:** MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY. Default: MM/DD/YYYY.
- **Language:** English (US) is currently the only supported language. Additional languages coming soon.

## Linked Accounts

View and manage your linked external bank accounts at Settings > Linked Accounts. You can link up to 5 external bank accounts for transfers. Linking requires micro-deposit verification (2 small deposits of $0.01-$0.99, takes 1-2 business days).

## Data Export

Download your data at Settings > Privacy > Export Data. You can export:
- Transaction history (CSV or PDF)
- Account statements (PDF)
- Tax documents (1099-INT, if applicable)
