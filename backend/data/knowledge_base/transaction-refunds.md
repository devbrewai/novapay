# Refunds

## Merchant Refunds

When a merchant issues a refund for a purchase, the refund is processed back to your Nova account. The timeline depends on the merchant and the payment method:

- **Debit card purchases:** 3-5 business days after the merchant initiates the refund
- **Online purchases:** 5-10 business days (some merchants take longer)
- **Subscription cancellations:** Depends on the merchant's refund policy. Most process within 5-7 business days.

## Nova Credits

In some cases, Nova may issue a direct credit to your account:
- Dispute resolution credits (when a dispute is resolved in your favor)
- Fee reversals (if you were charged a fee in error)
- Promotional credits (sign-up bonuses, referral rewards)
- Provisional credits (during an active dispute investigation)

Nova credits appear in your account instantly.

## Refund Status

Track refund status at Transactions > Filter > Refunds. Each refund shows:
- Original transaction details
- Refund amount
- Status: Pending, Processing, Completed
- Expected completion date

## Partial Refunds

Merchants can issue partial refunds. If you returned part of an order or negotiated a partial refund, the refunded amount will be less than the original charge. Both the original charge and the partial refund appear as separate line items in your transaction history.

## Refund Not Received?

If a merchant said they issued a refund but you haven't received it within 10 business days:
1. Check the transaction history for any pending refund entries
2. Contact the merchant to confirm the refund was processed and get a refund confirmation number
3. If the merchant confirms the refund was sent, contact Nova support with the confirmation number and we'll investigate
