# Direct Deposit

## Overview

Set up direct deposit with Nova to receive your paycheck, government benefits, or other recurring payments directly into your account. With Nova Early Access, you can get paid up to 2 days early.

## How to Set Up Direct Deposit

1. Go to Settings > Payments > Direct Deposit
2. You'll see your Nova routing number and account number
3. Share these details with your employer's payroll department, benefits provider, or the agency issuing your payments

**Your Nova banking details:**
- Routing number: 021-000-089
- Account number: Found at Settings > Payments > Direct Deposit (starts with 4821 for this account)

Most employers accept direct deposit setup through their payroll portal. Some may require a voided check or a direct deposit authorization form — you can download a pre-filled form from the Direct Deposit setup page.

## Early Access

Nova Early Access lets you receive your direct deposit up to 2 days before your scheduled payday. This feature is automatic — no setup required. When your employer sends the deposit instruction to the bank, Nova makes the funds available immediately instead of waiting for the standard settlement window.

Early Access works with most employers and payroll providers. The exact timing depends on when your employer submits the payroll file.

## Splitting Direct Deposits

You can split your direct deposit between multiple accounts:
- Set a fixed dollar amount to go to Nova, with the remainder to another account
- Set a percentage split between accounts
- Direct your full paycheck to Nova

Configure splits through your employer's payroll system, not through Nova.

## Switching From Another Bank

When switching your direct deposit to Nova, allow 1-2 payroll cycles for the change to take effect. Keep your old account open until you've confirmed the deposit is arriving in Nova.
