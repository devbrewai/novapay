# Nova Account Types

## Standard Account

The Standard account is free to open and maintain. There is no monthly fee and no minimum balance requirement.

**Standard account features:**
- Virtual debit card (instant)
- Physical debit card (free, standard shipping in 7-10 business days)
- Up to $5,000 daily transfer limit
- Up to $10,000 monthly transfer limit
- Free domestic transfers between Nova accounts
- 0.5% APY on balances up to $10,000
- Basic spending insights and transaction categorization
- Access to bill pay and direct deposit
- 1% cashback on select categories (dining, groceries)

## Premium Account

The Premium account costs $9.99/month and includes enhanced features for power users, freelancers, and small business owners.

**Premium account features (everything in Standard, plus):**
- Up to $25,000 daily transfer limit
- Up to $100,000 monthly transfer limit
- 2.0% APY on balances up to $50,000
- 2% cashback on all purchases (no category restrictions)
- Priority customer support (average response time under 2 minutes)
- Fee-free international transfers up to $5,000/month
- Free expedited card shipping (2-3 business days)
- Advanced spending analytics with monthly reports
- Savings goals with auto-save rules
- Free wire transfers (up to 5 per month)

## How to Check Your Account Type

Go to Settings > Account > Account Type. Your current tier is displayed at the top of the page.
