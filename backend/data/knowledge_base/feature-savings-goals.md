# Savings Goals (Premium)

## Overview

NovaPay Savings Goals help you save for specific purposes by setting aside money in dedicated goal buckets within your account. This feature is available to Premium account holders.

## Creating a Savings Goal

1. Go to Home > Savings Goals > Create Goal
2. Name your goal (e.g., "Vacation Fund", "Emergency Fund", "New Laptop")
3. Set your target amount
4. Set your target date (optional)
5. Choose how you want to fund it:
   - **Manual deposits:** Add money to the goal whenever you want
   - **Auto-save:** Set up automatic transfers on a schedule
   - **Round-ups:** Round up every purchase to the nearest dollar and save the difference

## Auto-Save Rules

Set up automatic contributions to your savings goals:
- **Recurring transfer:** Move a fixed amount (e.g., $50) on a schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
- **Percentage of income:** Automatically save a percentage of each direct deposit
- **Round-ups:** Every debit card purchase is rounded up to the nearest dollar. The difference is moved to your goal. Example: $3.75 coffee → $0.25 saved.

## Managing Your Goals

- **View progress:** Each goal shows a progress bar, current balance, and projected completion date
- **Add money:** Manually add to any goal at any time
- **Withdraw:** Move money from a goal back to your main balance instantly
- **Edit:** Change the goal name, target amount, or target date
- **Delete:** Remove a goal and return any saved funds to your main balance

## Limits

- Up to 10 active savings goals
- No minimum or maximum goal amount
- Savings goal balances are included in your total account balance
- Savings goal funds earn the same APY as your main balance (2.0% for Premium)

## Goal Insights

Track your savings momentum:
- Monthly savings rate
- Projected goal completion date based on current pace
- Streak tracker (consecutive weeks with a contribution)
