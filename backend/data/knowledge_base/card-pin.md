# Card PIN

## Setting Your PIN

You set your 4-digit PIN when you activate your physical card. Your PIN is used for ATM withdrawals and in-store purchases that require PIN entry.

## Changing Your PIN

To change your PIN at any time:
1. Go to Cards > select your card > Change PIN
2. Enter your current PIN
3. Enter your new 4-digit PIN
4. Confirm your new PIN
5. Your PIN is updated immediately

You can also change your PIN at any NovaPay-supported ATM.

## Forgot Your PIN?

If you've forgotten your PIN:
1. Go to Cards > select your card > Reset PIN
2. Verify your identity with biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint) or your account password
3. Set a new 4-digit PIN
4. Your new PIN takes effect immediately

## PIN Security Tips

- Never share your PIN with anyone, including NovaPay support (we will never ask for it)
- Don't use easily guessable numbers like 1234, 0000, or your birth year
- Use a different PIN than your other bank cards
- Cover the keypad when entering your PIN at ATMs or point-of-sale terminals

## PIN Lockout

For security, your card is temporarily locked after 3 consecutive incorrect PIN attempts. The lockout lasts 30 minutes, after which you can try again. If you continue to enter the wrong PIN, the card will be locked and you'll need to contact support to unlock it.

## ATM Usage

Your NovaPay PIN works at any Visa-compatible ATM. Free withdrawals at 55,000+ Allpoint ATMs nationwide. Out-of-network ATM fee: $2.50 (Standard) or free up to 5/month (Premium). The ATM operator may charge an additional fee.
