# Mobile App

## Download

The NovaPay app is available for free:
- **iPhone:** Download from the App Store (requires iOS 16 or later)
- **Android:** Download from the Google Play Store (requires Android 12 or later)

## Key Features

### Dashboard
- View your account balance and available balance at a glance
- See recent transactions
- Quick access to Send, Request, and Pay Bills

### Transactions
- Full transaction history with search and filters
- Filter by date range, category, amount, or status
- Tap any transaction for details, dispute options, or category editing

### Cards
- View and manage your virtual and physical cards
- Freeze/unfreeze cards instantly
- View card details (number, CVV, expiration)
- Order replacement cards
- Set spending limits and merchant controls

### Send Money
- Send money to other NovaPay users, external bank accounts, or internationally
- Save frequent recipients for quick transfers
- Schedule future or recurring transfers

### Pay Bills
- Pay bills to thousands of billers
- Set up recurring bill payments
- View payment history and upcoming scheduled payments

### Settings
- Update personal information
- Manage security settings (password, 2FA, biometrics)
- Configure notification preferences
- View and manage linked accounts
- Access statements and tax documents

## Mobile Check Deposit

Deposit checks using your phone's camera:
1. Go to Deposit > Check
2. Enter the check amount
3. Take a photo of the front and back of the check
4. Submit for processing

Limits: $5,000/day (Standard), $10,000/day (Premium). Funds are typically available within 1 business day.

## Biometric Login

Enable Face ID or fingerprint authentication for quick, secure access. Go to Settings > Security > Biometric Login after your first password login.

## App Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Automatic session timeout after 30 days of inactivity
- Remote session termination from any device
- No sensitive data stored on device (card numbers, SSN, etc.)
