# Suspicious Activity

## How NovaPay Monitors Your Account

NovaPay uses advanced fraud detection to monitor your account 24/7. Our system analyzes transaction patterns, locations, merchant types, and amounts to identify potentially fraudulent activity.

## Types of Suspicious Activity

NovaPay may flag the following:
- Transactions in locations you've never been to
- Multiple high-value transactions in a short time
- Purchases at high-risk merchant categories
- Login attempts from unfamiliar devices or locations
- Transactions that don't match your typical spending patterns

## What Happens When Suspicious Activity Is Detected

1. **Transaction hold:** The suspicious transaction may be temporarily held or declined
2. **Notification:** You'll receive a push notification and/or SMS asking you to verify the transaction
3. **Verification options:**
   - Confirm it was you (the transaction proceeds)
   - Report it as unauthorized (the transaction is blocked and your card may be frozen)

## What to Do If You Notice Suspicious Activity

If you see transactions you don't recognize:

1. **Immediately freeze your card:** Cards > select card > Freeze Card
2. **Report the unauthorized transaction:** Tap the transaction > "Report as Unauthorized"
3. **Change your password:** Settings > Security > Change Password
4. **Check your 2FA settings:** Ensure no unauthorized devices have been added
5. **Contact support:** Chat with Nova or call 1-800-NOVAPAY for immediate assistance

## False Positives

Sometimes legitimate transactions are flagged as suspicious, especially when:
- You're traveling and making purchases in a new location
- You make a large purchase that's unusual for your account
- You use your card at a new type of merchant

If your transaction is declined due to fraud detection, simply verify it through the notification you receive and the transaction will be allowed to proceed.

## Fraud Protection Guarantee

NovaPay's fraud protection covers 100% of unauthorized transactions, provided they are reported within 60 days. You will never be held liable for transactions you didn't authorize.
