# Transfer Limits

## Overview

Nova enforces transfer limits to protect your account and comply with federal regulations. Limits vary by account type and transfer method.

## Standard Account Limits

| Transfer Method | Per Transaction | Daily | Monthly |
|----------------|-----------------|-------|---------|
| Internal (Nova to Nova) | $5,000 | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| Domestic ACH | $5,000 | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| International | $2,500 | $2,500 | $5,000 |
| Wire transfer | $5,000 | $5,000 | $10,000 |

## Premium Account Limits

| Transfer Method | Per Transaction | Daily | Monthly |
|----------------|-----------------|-------|---------|
| Internal (Nova to Nova) | $25,000 | $25,000 | $100,000 |
| Domestic ACH | $25,000 | $25,000 | $100,000 |
| International | $10,000 | $10,000 | $50,000 |
| Wire transfer | $25,000 | $25,000 | $100,000 |

## Requesting Higher Limits

If you need to make a transfer that exceeds your current limits:

1. Go to Settings > Account > Limits > Request Increase
2. Select the limit you want to increase and the desired amount
3. Provide a brief explanation for the increase
4. Our team reviews requests within 1 business day

Temporary increases (up to 2x your normal limit) can be granted for up to 30 days. Permanent increases may require enhanced verification, including proof of income or business documentation.

## Why Limits Exist

Transfer limits help prevent unauthorized transactions, comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, and protect your funds. If you notice a transfer you did not authorize, contact support immediately.
