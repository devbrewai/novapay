# Virtual Cards

## Overview

Every NovaPay account comes with a virtual debit card that's ready to use immediately after account activation. Virtual cards work for online purchases, in-app payments, and contactless payments via Apple Pay or Google Pay.

## Card Details

Your virtual card has its own unique 16-digit card number, expiration date, and CVV. View your virtual card details at Cards > Virtual Card > Show Details. For security, the full card number is hidden by default and requires biometric or PIN authentication to reveal.

## How to Use Your Virtual Card

- **Online shopping:** Enter the card number, expiration date, and CVV at checkout
- **Apple Pay / Google Pay:** Add your virtual card to your mobile wallet for contactless payments in stores
- **In-app purchases:** Use wherever Visa debit cards are accepted
- **Subscription services:** Perfect for signing up for streaming services, software subscriptions, and more

## Generating Additional Virtual Cards

Premium account holders can generate up to 5 additional virtual cards. This is useful for:
- Separating personal and business spending
- Using a dedicated card for subscriptions (easy to cancel if needed)
- Creating temporary cards for one-time purchases

To generate a new virtual card: Cards > Add Virtual Card > Confirm.

## Freezing Your Virtual Card

If you suspect unauthorized use, you can instantly freeze your virtual card: Cards > Virtual Card > Freeze Card. When frozen, all transactions will be declined. You can unfreeze at any time.

## Virtual Card Limits

Virtual cards share the same limits as your account. There are no separate limits for virtual vs. physical cards.
