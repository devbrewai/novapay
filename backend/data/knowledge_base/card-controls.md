# Card Controls

## Overview

Nova gives you full control over how your debit cards are used. Access card controls at Cards > select a card > Card Controls.

## Freeze and Unfreeze

Instantly freeze any card to block all transactions. Useful if you've misplaced your card or suspect unauthorized use. Frozen cards decline all purchases, ATM withdrawals, and online transactions. Recurring payments set up before freezing may still process. Unfreeze at any time to resume normal use.

## Spending Limits

Set custom spending limits for each card:
- **Per-transaction limit:** Maximum amount for a single purchase (e.g., $200)
- **Daily limit:** Maximum total spending per day
- **Monthly limit:** Maximum total spending per month

These limits are in addition to your account-level limits. The lower of the two applies.

## Merchant Category Controls

Block or allow specific merchant categories:
- **ATM withdrawals:** Block cash withdrawals
- **Online purchases:** Block e-commerce transactions
- **International transactions:** Block purchases outside the US
- **Gambling:** Block gambling and lottery merchants
- **Adult content:** Block adult entertainment merchants

Toggle each category on or off. Changes take effect immediately.

## Transaction Notifications

Enable real-time notifications for every transaction:
- Push notification with merchant name, amount, and location
- Option to instantly report unauthorized transactions
- Customizable alert threshold (only notify for transactions above a certain amount)

## Location-Based Controls (Premium)

Premium accounts can enable location-based security:
- Allow transactions only in your current geographic area
- Automatically freeze card when you're not near your phone
- Set up trusted locations (home, office) where transactions are always allowed
