# Account Limits

## Transfer Limits

Transfer limits depend on your account type and verification status.

### Standard Account

| Transfer Type | Daily Limit | Monthly Limit |
|---------------|-------------|---------------|
| Domestic ACH | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| Internal (Nova to Nova) | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| International | $2,500 | $5,000 |
| Wire transfer | $5,000 | $10,000 |

### Premium Account

| Transfer Type | Daily Limit | Monthly Limit |
|---------------|-------------|---------------|
| Domestic ACH | $25,000 | $100,000 |
| Internal (Nova to Nova) | $25,000 | $100,000 |
| International | $10,000 | $50,000 |
| Wire transfer | $25,000 | $100,000 |

## ATM Withdrawal Limits

- Standard: $500/day, $2,000/month
- Premium: $1,000/day, $5,000/month
- ATM fees: Free at 55,000+ Allpoint ATMs. $2.50 fee at out-of-network ATMs. Premium accounts get up to 5 out-of-network ATM fee reimbursements per month.

## Purchase Limits

- Standard: $5,000 per single transaction, $10,000/day
- Premium: $10,000 per single transaction, $25,000/day

## Requesting a Limit Increase

If you need higher limits temporarily or permanently:

1. Go to Settings > Account > Limits
2. Tap "Request Limit Increase"
3. Select the type of limit you want to increase
4. Provide a reason for the increase
5. Requests are reviewed within 1 business day

Temporary limit increases (up to 2x your current limit) can be approved for up to 30 days. Permanent increases may require additional verification.

## Deposit Limits

- Mobile check deposit: $5,000/day, $10,000/month (Standard); $10,000/day, $25,000/month (Premium)
- Direct deposit: No limit
- ACH deposit from external bank: $25,000/day for all account types
