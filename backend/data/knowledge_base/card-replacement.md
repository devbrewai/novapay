# Card Replacement

## When to Replace Your Card

Request a card replacement if:
- Your card is lost or stolen
- Your card is damaged (chip not reading, magnetic stripe worn)
- Your card is expiring soon
- You want a new card number (after fraud or unauthorized use)

## How to Request a Replacement

1. Go to Cards > select the affected card > Replace Card
2. Select the reason for replacement
3. Choose whether you need a new card number:
   - **Same number:** Your existing card number is kept. Best for damaged cards.
   - **New number:** A completely new card number is issued. Best for lost/stolen cards or after fraud. Note: you'll need to update your card on file with any merchants or subscription services.
4. Confirm your mailing address
5. Select shipping speed

## Shipping Options

- **Standard (free):** 7-10 business days
- **Expedited ($10, free for Premium):** 2-3 business days
- **Emergency (Premium only, $25):** Next business day delivery

## What Happens to Your Old Card

- **Lost/stolen:** The old card is immediately deactivated. Any pending transactions may still process.
- **Damaged:** The old card remains active until your replacement arrives and is activated.
- **Fraud:** The old card is immediately deactivated and a fraud investigation is opened.

## Temporary Virtual Card

While waiting for your replacement physical card, your virtual card remains fully functional. If you reported your card as stolen and need a new card number, a new virtual card with the new number is generated instantly.

## Fees

Card replacement is free for Standard and Premium accounts, regardless of the reason. Only shipping upgrades (expedited or emergency) incur a fee.
