# Upgrading to Premium

## Why Upgrade?

The Nova Premium account is designed for freelancers, small business owners, and power users who need higher limits, better rewards, and priority support.

**Key benefits of upgrading:**
- 5x higher daily transfer limit ($25,000 vs $5,000)
- 2% cashback on all purchases (vs 1% on select categories)
- 2.0% APY on balances up to $50,000 (vs 0.5% on $10,000)
- Fee-free international transfers up to $5,000/month
- Priority customer support with average response time under 2 minutes
- Free wire transfers (up to 5/month)
- Advanced spending analytics and monthly reports
- Savings goals with auto-save rules

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to Settings > Account > Account Type
2. Tap "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Review the Premium features and $9.99/month pricing
4. Confirm your upgrade

Your upgrade takes effect immediately. Your first month is prorated based on the day you upgrade. The monthly fee is charged on the first of each month.

## Free Trial

New Nova users are eligible for a 30-day free trial of Premium. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, you will be charged $9.99/month going forward.

## Downgrading Back to Standard

You can downgrade at any time at Settings > Account > Account Type > Downgrade to Standard.

**What happens when you downgrade:**
- Your account limits revert to Standard levels
- Cashback rate changes from 2% to 1% on select categories
- APY drops from 2.0% to 0.5%
- Any pending wire transfers will still process
- You keep any cashback rewards earned during your Premium period
- The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle

## Annual Plan

Pay annually and save: $99.99/year ($8.33/month) instead of $119.88/year ($9.99/month). That's a 17% savings. Switch to annual billing at Settings > Account > Billing.
