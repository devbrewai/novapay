# Bill Pay

## Overview

Nova Bill Pay lets you pay bills directly from your account to thousands of billers across the United States. Pay utilities, rent, credit cards, insurance, and more without leaving the Nova app.

## Setting Up Bill Pay

1. Go to Pay Bills > Add Biller
2. Search for your biller by name (e.g., "Con Edison", "AT&T", "State Farm")
3. Enter your account number with the biller
4. Confirm the biller details

## Paying a Bill

1. Go to Pay Bills > select a biller
2. Enter the payment amount
3. Select the payment date (today or schedule for a future date)
4. Review and confirm

## Payment Methods

- **Electronic payment (most billers):** Processed in 1-2 business days. Free.
- **Paper check (when electronic is not available):** Nova mails a physical check on your behalf. Allow 5-7 business days. Free.

## Scheduled and Recurring Payments

Set up automatic bill payments:
1. When paying a bill, toggle "Make this recurring"
2. Select the frequency (monthly, bi-weekly, etc.)
3. Set the payment amount (fixed amount or "pay full balance" if the biller supports it)
4. Choose the start date

## Bill Pay Limits

- Minimum payment: $1
- Maximum single payment: $10,000 (Standard), $25,000 (Premium)
- Maximum monthly bill pay total: $25,000 (Standard), $100,000 (Premium)

## Cancelling a Payment

Cancel a scheduled payment before it's processed: Pay Bills > Scheduled > select payment > Cancel. Electronic payments can be cancelled up to 1 business day before the scheduled date. Paper check payments can be cancelled before the check is mailed.

## Supported Billers

Nova supports thousands of billers including major utilities, telecoms, insurance companies, credit card issuers, government agencies, and more. If your biller is not listed, you can pay via paper check using their mailing address.
