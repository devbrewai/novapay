# Physical Cards

## Ordering Your Card

Every Nova account is eligible for a free physical debit card.

**To order your card:**
1. Go to Cards > Order Physical Card
2. Confirm your mailing address
3. Choose your shipping speed:
   - **Standard (free):** 7-10 business days
   - **Expedited ($10, free for Premium):** 2-3 business days

## Activating Your Card

When your card arrives:
1. Open the Nova app
2. Go to Cards > Activate Card
3. Enter the last 4 digits of your card number
4. Set your 4-digit PIN
5. Your card is now active and ready to use

## Card Features

- **Visa debit card:** Accepted at millions of merchants worldwide
- **Contactless payments:** Tap to pay at supported terminals
- **ATM access:** Withdraw cash at any Visa-compatible ATM
- **Chip and PIN:** Enhanced security for in-person purchases
- **No annual fee:** Your physical card is free to use

## Card Design

Nova cards feature a sleek, minimalist design with your name embossed on the front. The card number is printed on the back for added security.

## Card Expiration

Physical cards are valid for 3 years. A replacement card is automatically mailed to your address on file 30 days before expiration. Your card number remains the same unless you request a new one.

## Limits

Your physical card shares the same limits as your account and virtual card. ATM-specific limits: Standard $500/day, Premium $1,000/day.
