# Notifications

## Overview

Nova sends notifications to keep you informed about your account activity, security events, and product updates. Manage your notification preferences at Settings > Notifications.

## Notification Types

### Transaction Alerts
- **Every transaction:** Get notified for every purchase, transfer, or deposit
- **Large transactions only:** Set a custom threshold (e.g., $50, $100, $500) and only get notified for transactions above that amount
- **Declined transactions:** Get notified when a transaction is declined

### Security Alerts (cannot be disabled)
- Login from a new device
- Password changed
- 2FA settings modified
- Card frozen or unfrozen
- Suspicious activity detected

### Account Alerts
- Low balance warning (set a custom threshold, e.g., alert when balance drops below $100)
- Direct deposit received
- Refund received
- Recurring payment processed
- Bill payment processed or failed

### Savings Alerts (Premium)
- Savings goal milestone reached (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- Auto-save contribution processed
- Savings streak achievement

### Weekly Summary
- Sent every Monday morning
- Total spending for the past week
- Top spending categories
- Comparison to previous week

## Notification Channels

For each notification type, choose how you want to be notified:
- **Push notification:** Real-time alert on your phone
- **Email:** Sent to your registered email address
- **SMS:** Text message to your registered phone number

You can enable multiple channels for the same notification type.

## Quiet Hours

Set quiet hours to mute non-urgent notifications during specific times (e.g., 10 PM to 7 AM). Security alerts are always delivered regardless of quiet hours. Go to Settings > Notifications > Quiet Hours.
